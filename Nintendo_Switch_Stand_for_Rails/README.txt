# Summary

A slim stand for holding the switch while allowing access to the charging port. This design uses the rails for stability, which allows the legs to be small enough to store in a case. It also has a small ridge to hold the Switch from the bottom. To remove the stand, just slightly pull the leg away from the Switch, and slide off.

45, 55, 60, and 65 degree angles available. You will need to print both a right and left leg.

Designed from scratch based on  andyleer's 45 degree stand.

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: FlashForge
Printer: Creator Pro
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.25mm
Infill: 20%

# Post-Printing

Sand the rail sections on the legs smooth to allow easy insertion into the switch. Never use force when inserting into the Switch to avoid damaging it.
